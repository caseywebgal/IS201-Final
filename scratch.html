Hobbits | Elves | Dwarves

Characteristics of LOTR Species



  1. Hobbits
    • Very Short
    • Large Hairy Feet
    • Hate Adventure (most of the time)
    • Eat Large Ammounts
    • Cheery Folk
    • Lives in the Shire
    • Mostly Farmers
  2. Elves
    • Immortal, unless comething kills them
    • Very Tall
    • Known for Their Beauty
    • Noble People
    • Family Focused
    • Can be Aloof at times
    • Long Hair with Decoritive Braids
    • Can Hold Grudges for Eternity
    • Hates Dwarves
  3. Dwarves
    • Taller Than Hobbits but Still Very Short
    • Stocky Build
    • Long Beards
    • Work in Mines
    • Known to be Stubborn
    • Favor Axes and Hammers for Battle
    • Loyal To a Fault
    • Hates Elves
